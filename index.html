General Motors

2023
Website Strategy Design System

General Motors, one of the world's largest automakers, relied on separate agency partners to manage each brand — resulting in fragmented UX and code. I led the Aurora design system to unify the experience across the organization, then applied it to redesign brand sites for Cadillac, GMC, Chevrolet, and Buick — covering the full ownership journey from exploring vehicles and shopping, to onboarding, account management, support, and a community for enthusiasts and DIYers.
